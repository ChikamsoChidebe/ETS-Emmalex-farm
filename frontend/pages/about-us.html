Donkey Farming Excellence

Specialized Agricultural Innovation

ETS EMMALEX Global Limited Farm is a specialized farm that focuses on donkey farming excellence. We operate across 40 hectares of pristine land dedicated to pasturing and grass cultivation. Our world-class standard processing facility features an integrated mini ranch, representing the pinnacle of modern agricultural infrastructure.

Our mission extends far beyond providing employment opportunities for the local community. We are committed to establishing a sustainable economic ecosystem through innovative donkey farming practices that benefit both our region and the broader agricultural sector.

Cocoa Excellence

Sustainable Cocoa Production

Nestled in the lush, tropical regions of West Africa, our successful cocoa farm stands as a shining example of productivity and sustainability in cocoa production. This multi-generational operation has flourished for decades, earning recognition for high-quality cocoa beans and responsible farming practices.

At the heart of our cocoa farm's success is an unwavering commitment to sustainable farming practices. We employ organic methods to maintain soil health and minimize chemical usage. Our dedication extends to preserving biodiversity, protecting local ecosystems, and implementing responsible processing methods.

Our Products

Premium Quality Products

Premium Donkey Breeds

ETS EMMALEX Global Limited Farm produces donkeys of different breeds, such as Miniature Mediterranean, American Mammoth, Appaloosa Horses, Standard Donkey, and Baudet du Potiu. Our donkeys are properly trained and well-fed.

Premium Donkey Meat

ETS EMMALEX Global Limited Farm produces donkey meat that can be considered a good alternative to red meat option, being a dietary meat. Donkey meat is characterized by low fat, low cholesterol content, a favourable fatty acid profile, and is rich in iron.

Fresh Donkey Milk

ETS EMMALEX Global Limited Farm produces donkey milk, which helps promote the growth of good bacteria. It has been used in some countries as a folk medicine for viruses like whooping cough. Donkey milk can also prompt the release of nitric oxide.

Our Journey

Our History

ETS EMMALEX Global Limited Farm is a donkey breeding project with over 15 to 20 years of experience in donkey farming. Throughout our journey, ETS EMMALEX Global Farm has engaged in the breeding of good-quality donkeys to produce subsequent generations of donkeys that are highly productive with enhanced overall performance that will augment further production and profitability.

Over the past years, we have also engaged in the production of donkey products such as milk, oil, skin, and meat, which have been of great benefit to both the nation and foreign countries at large. The Animal Breeders and Skin Guild Association of Nigeria has also proposed investment in our farm to enhance the breeding of more donkeys in Nigeria.
