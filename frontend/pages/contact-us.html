FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our farm, products, and services.

What products do you offer?

We offer premium donkey products including live donkeys, donkey milk, donkey meat, and high-quality cocoa products. All our products meet international quality standards.

Can I visit your farm?

Yes! We welcome visitors for educational tours and farm excursions. Please contact us in advance to schedule your visit and ensure the best experience.

Do you offer delivery services?

Yes, we provide reliable delivery services across Nigeria and can arrange international shipping for our products. Contact us for specific delivery options and pricing.

What makes your donkeys special?

Our donkeys are carefully bred for quality, health, and temperament. We maintain different breeds including Baudet du Poitou, Miniature Mediterranean, and American Mammoth Jackstock, all with proper health certificates.

How can I place an order?

You can place orders by calling us, sending a WhatsApp message, or using our booking form. We'll discuss your requirements and provide detailed information about availability and pricing.

Do you offer partnerships?

Yes, we're open to partnerships and collaborations. We work with NADFON and are interested in expanding our network with serious agricultural partners.
