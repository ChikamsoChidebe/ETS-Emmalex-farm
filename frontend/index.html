Our Expertise

15+ Years of Donkey Farming Excellence

ETS EMMALEX Global Limited Farm stands as Nigeria's premier donkey farming specialist, operating across 40 hectares of pristine pastureland. Our world-class facilities include a standard processing house with an integrated mini ranch, setting new benchmarks in agricultural excellence.

Beyond employment generation, we're committed to building a sustainable agro-economy through innovative donkey farming practices. Our contribution to Nigeria's agricultural sector spans employment creation, economic development, and international trade partnerships.

Our breeding programs focus on producing superior donkey generations with enhanced productivity and performance. Over 15-20 years, we've mastered the production of premium donkey products including milk, oil, skin, and meat, benefiting both domestic and international markets.

Cocoa Excellence

Sustainable Cocoa Farming Heritage

Nestled in West Africa's lush tropical regions, our cocoa farm exemplifies productivity and sustainability in modern cocoa production. This multi-generational operation has flourished for decades, earning recognition for exceptional quality beans and responsible farming practices.

Our commitment to sustainable farming drives every aspect of our operation. We employ organic methods to maintain soil health, minimize chemical usage, and preserve biodiversity. Our environmental stewardship extends to water conservation and waste reduction through responsible processing methods.

We cultivate premium cocoa varieties renowned for their exceptional flavor profiles. Each cocoa tree receives meticulous care to ensure the highest quality pods. Our expert team carefully handpicks ripe pods and employs traditional fermentation and drying techniques, producing rich, aromatic cocoa sought after by artisanal chocolate makers worldwide.
